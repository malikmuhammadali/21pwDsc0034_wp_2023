Malam Jabba

Malam Jabba is a popular ski resort in Swat Valley, known for its stunning natural beauty and excellent skiing facilities. The resort offers breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains, making it a paradise for winter sports enthusiasts. Don't miss the opportunity to experience skiing, snowboarding, and tubing in this picturesque location.

Mingora

Mingora, the largest city in Swat, serves as a gateway to the valley's numerous attractions. It offers a blend of modern amenities and natural beauty. You can explore local markets, taste delicious Swati cuisine, and visit archaeological sites, such as Udegram Buddhist Monastery.

Kalam Valley

Kalam Valley, a picturesque gem in Pakistan's Swat region, captivates with its pristine landscapes, lush meadows, and serene rivers. This tranquil haven offers an ideal escape for nature lovers and hikers, boasting scenic beauty at every turn. Whether trekking through dense forests or embracing the warmth of the local community, Kalam Valley promises a serene retreat from urban life.
